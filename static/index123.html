Key facts

Redcliffe Partners was founded in December 2015 as the successor firm to Clifford Chance in Ukraine. All lawyers and business services staff of Clifford Chance in Ukraine remained with us as part of the succession.

The firm was founded by Olexiy Soshenko, Dmytro Fedoruk and Yehven Deyneko. They are the former heads of Banking & Finance, Corporate/M&A and Antitrust practices of Clifford Chance in Ukraine.

The firm focuses primarily on Banking & Finance, Corporate/M&A and Antitrust. We are also regularly involved in real estate, commercial, regulatory, international arbitration and domestic litigation.

1 October 2008

Clifford Chance opens its Ukrainian office.

Oct. 2008 — Dec. 2015

Clifford Chance quickly builds the reputation as one of the leading law firms working on high-end deals on the Ukrainian market.

1 December 2015

Our office becomes independent from Clifford Chance and changes the name to Redcliffe Partners.
